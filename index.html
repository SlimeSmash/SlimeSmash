Introduction

SlimeSmash Studios ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect information when you use our mobile game SlimeSmash (the "Game").

Data Controller: SlimeSmash Studios
Contact: SlimeSmash.Dev@gmail.com

By using the Game, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the Game.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information Collected Automatically

We collect the following data automatically when you use the Game:

  • Device Identifiers: Advertising ID, device model, operating system version, and app version for authentication and analytics
  • Gameplay Data: Progress, levels completed, coins earned, achievements unlocked, and in-game statistics
  • Technical Data: IP address (anonymized), crash reports, error logs, performance metrics, and diagnostic information
  • Usage Data: Session duration, features used, and interaction patterns to improve game balance

1.2 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: If you sign in via Google Play Games or similar services, we receive your service ID and public profile information (such as username/gamertag)
  • Support Requests: Information you provide when contacting customer support

1.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect:

  • Real names, physical addresses, or phone numbers
  • Precise geolocation data
  • Financial or payment information (any purchases are handled directly by app stores)
  • Sensitive personal data (health, biometric, racial/ethnic origin, political opinions, religious beliefs, etc.)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information for the following purposes:

  • Game Functionality: Enable cloud saves, cross-device progress synchronization, and account authentication
  • Leaderboards & Social Features: Display rankings, achievements, and enable competitive features
  • Game Improvement: Analyze gameplay patterns to improve balance, difficulty, and user experience
  • Technical Operations: Monitor performance, fix bugs, prevent crashes, and maintain security
  • Fraud Prevention: Detect and prevent cheating, unauthorized access, or other violations of our Terms of Service
  • Remote Configuration: Update game settings, events, and features without requiring app updates
  • Legal Compliance: Comply with legal obligations and enforce our policies

Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR):

  • Performance of contract (providing game services)
  • Legitimate interests (improving our services, fraud prevention, analytics)
  • Consent (where required by law)
  • Legal obligation (compliance with laws)

3. Third-Party Services & Data Sharing

We use third-party service providers to help operate the Game. These services may collect and process data on our behalf:

3.1 Service Providers

  • Google Play Services – Authentication, achievements, leaderboards, and cloud save
    Data shared: Device identifiers, gameplay data, account ID
  • Unity Gaming Services – Cloud save, authentication, analytics, and remote configuration
    Data shared: Device identifiers, technical data, usage statistics

3.2 Data Sharing Practices

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share data only in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: With third parties listed above who process data on our behalf under strict contractual obligations
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or government request
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets (you will be notified)
  • Protection of Rights: To protect our rights, property, safety, or that of our users

3.3 International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries outside your residence, including the United States. We ensure adequate safeguards are in place, such as:

  • Standard Contractual Clauses approved by the European Commission
  • Privacy Shield Framework (where applicable)
  • Service providers certified under recognized data protection programs

4. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

4.1 Rights for All Users

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data (see Section 5 below)
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Withdrawal of Consent: Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent

4.2 Additional Rights (GDPR - EU/EEA Users)

  • Data Portability: Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Restriction: Request restriction of processing in certain circumstances
  • Objection: Object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Automated Decision-Making: Not be subject to decisions based solely on automated processing (we do not engage in such processing)
  • Lodge a Complaint: File a complaint with your local data protection authority

4.3 California Privacy Rights (CCPA/CPRA)

California residents have additional rights:

  • Know what personal information is collected, used, shared, or sold
  • Request deletion of personal information
  • Opt-out of the sale of personal information (note: we do not sell personal information)
  • Non-discrimination for exercising these rights
To Exercise Your Rights: Contact us at SlimeSmash.Dev@gmail.com or use the in-app deletion feature (Settings → Delete Account). We will respond within 30 days for GDPR requests and 45 days for CCPA requests.

5. Data Deletion

You have full control over your data and may delete it permanently at any time:

5.1 In-App Deletion

Navigate to Settings → Delete Account within the Game. This will immediately and permanently delete:

  • All cloud-saved game progress
  • Leaderboard entries and achievements
  • Account identifiers and associated data

5.2 Email Request

Send a deletion request to SlimeSmash.Dev@gmail.com with the subject "Data Deletion Request" and include your user ID or account identifier. We will process your request within 7 business days.

5.3 Important Notes

  • Deletion is permanent and irreversible – all game progress will be lost
  • Local data stored on your device must be deleted by uninstalling the app
  • Some anonymized analytics data may be retained for legal or operational purposes
  • We may retain certain data if required by law or for legitimate legal purposes

6. Data Security & Retention

6.1 Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • Encryption: All data transmissions use HTTPS/TLS encryption
  • Access Controls: Strict internal access controls and authentication
  • Secure Storage: Data stored on secure servers with regular security audits
  • Monitoring: Continuous monitoring for security threats and vulnerabilities

However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

6.2 Data Retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary for the purposes outlined in this policy:

  • Active accounts: Retained while you use the Game and for up to 12 months after last activity
  • Deleted accounts: Permanently deleted within 7 days of deletion request
  • Analytics data: Aggregated and anonymized data may be retained indefinitely for statistical purposes
  • Legal obligations: Data required for legal compliance may be retained as required by law

7. Children's Privacy

SlimeSmash does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13 (or the applicable age of digital consent in your jurisdiction). The Game is intended for general audiences and does not target children.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately at SlimeSmash.Dev@gmail.com. We will promptly investigate and delete such information.

Parents can learn more about protecting children's privacy online at the FTC's COPPA guidance.

8. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

SlimeSmash does not use browser cookies as it is a mobile application. However, our third-party service providers (Google Play Services, Unity) may use similar tracking technologies such as:

  • SDK Identifiers: To track sessions and usage patterns
  • Analytics Tools: To collect performance and behavioral data
  • Advertising IDs: Device advertising identifiers for analytics (not for targeted advertising)

You can reset your advertising identifier in your device settings:

  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Reset advertising ID
  • iOS: Settings → Privacy → Tracking (or Apple Advertising)
