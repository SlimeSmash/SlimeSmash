SlimeSmash Privacy Policy

Effective date: January 2026

SlimeSmash ("we," "our," or "the app") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting it. This policy explains how we collect, use, and handle data in our game.

1. Data We Collect

SlimeSmash does not collect personal information such as your name, email, address, or phone number.

We collect anonymous data for the purpose of improving the game experience:

• Crash logs — used to detect and fix bugs or crashes.
• Diagnostics — used to monitor app performance and stability.
• Other app performance data — helps us understand how the app runs on different devices.
• Device or other IDs — a random identifier for cloud save and leaderboard functionality.

All data is collected anonymously and cannot identify individual users.

2. How We Use the Data

We use the data collected for:

• Analytics — to understand how the game is played and how it performs.
• Fraud prevention and security — to detect unusual activity or cheating in leaderboards.
• Game improvement — fixing bugs, improving performance, and enhancing user experience.

3. Data Sharing

We do not share your data with third parties for advertising or other purposes.

The data collected is only sent to Unity Analytics and Unity Cloud Save, which help us monitor and improve the game.

4. Data Security

All data transmitted from your device is encrypted in transit.

We follow industry best practices to keep user data secure.

5. Data Deletion

Currently, users cannot request deletion of data.

All collected data is anonymous and only used for analytics and leaderboard functionality.

6. Children's Privacy

SlimeSmash is designed for users aged 5–15.

We comply with Google Play Families policy and applicable laws, including:

• US Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA)
• EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) for children

No personal information is collected from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page.
