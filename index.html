SlimeSmash Privacy Policy

Effective date: January 2026

1. Data We Collect

SlimeSmash does not collect personal information such as your name, email, address, or phone number.

We collect anonymous data for the purpose of improving the game experience, including:

  • Crash logs — detect and fix bugs or crashes
  • Diagnostics — monitor app performance and stability
  • Other app performance data — understand how the app runs on different devices
  • Device or other IDs — random identifier for cloud save and leaderboard functionality

All data is anonymous and cannot identify individual users.

2. How We Use the Data

  • Analytics — understand gameplay and performance
  • Fraud prevention & security — detect unusual activity or cheating
  • Game improvement — fix bugs, enhance performance, and improve user experience

3. Data Sharing

We do not share your data with third parties for advertising or other purposes.

Collected data is only sent to Unity Analytics and Unity Cloud Save to help monitor and improve the game.

4. Data Security

All data transmitted from your device is encrypted in transit. We follow industry best practices to keep user data secure.

5. Data Deletion

Currently, users cannot request deletion of data. All collected data is anonymous and used only for analytics and leaderboard functionality.

6. Children’s Privacy

SlimeSmash is intended for users aged 13 and older. No personal information is knowingly collected from children under 13. If you believe your child under 13 has provided personal information, please contact us to remove it.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page.
