Privacy Policy • Effective Jan 2026

1. Data We Collect

We believe in privacy by default. SlimeSmash does not collect personal info such as name, email, address, or phone number.

Anonymous data collected to elevate your gaming experience:

  • Crash Logs — Detect & fix bugs instantly.
  • Diagnostics — Monitor app performance & stability.
  • Performance Data — Optimize across devices.
  • Device IDs — Random identifiers for cloud saves & leaderboards.

2. How We Use Data

  • Analytics — Understanding gameplay patterns.
  • Security — Fraud prevention & anti-cheat measures.
  • Evolution — Continuous bug fixes & UX enhancements.

3. Data Sharing

We do not share data with third parties for advertising.

Anonymous data sent securely to Unity Analytics & Unity Cloud Save strictly for game functionality.

4. Data Security

All data is encrypted in transit using industry-standard protocols.

5. Data Deletion

All collected data is anonymous and tied only to gameplay features (leaderboards). Individual deletion requests cannot be processed.

6. Children's Privacy

Designed for ages 6–17. Fully compliant with Google Play Families Policy.

  • COPPA — US Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act
  • GDPR — EU General Data Protection Regulation for children
  • Zero personal information is collected from children under 13.

7. Policy Updates

Any updates to this privacy policy will be reflected immediately on this page.
