Privacy Policy • Jan 2026

1. Information We Collect

SlimeSmash collects only limited data required to operate and improve the game. We do not collect real-world personal information such as names, physical addresses, or phone numbers.

  • Account & Device Identifiers: Used for authentication, cloud saving, and leaderboards.
  • Gameplay Data: Progress, levels completed, coins, and similar in-game activity.
  • Diagnostics & Crash Data: Anonymous technical data to improve stability and performance.

2. How We Use Data

  • Enable cloud saves and cross-device progress
  • Provide leaderboards and achievements
  • Improve gameplay balance and performance
  • Detect cheating or unauthorized activity
  • Adjust gameplay settings and messaging using remote configuration without requiring app updates

3. Third-Party Services

  • Google Play Services – Authentication, achievements, and leaderboards
  • Unity Gaming Services – Cloud save, authentication, and analytics

4. Data Deletion

You have full control over your data and may delete it permanently at any time:

In-App: Use Settings → Delete Account to instantly remove cloud data.

Email Request: Contact us using the email below. Deletion requests are processed within 7 days.

Deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.

5. Security & Retention

All data is transmitted using HTTPS/TLS encryption. Player data is retained only as long as necessary to provide game services or until a deletion request is made.

6. Children’s Privacy

SlimeSmash does not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe such data has been collected, please contact us and it will be deleted promptly.
